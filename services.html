Hands-On Care

Manual Therapy

Skilled, hands-on techniques to restore mobility, reduce pain, and accelerate healing.

Book a Session
💉

Dry Needling

Thin needles inserted into trigger points to release muscle tension, reduce pain, and improve function. Highly effective for chronic muscle tightness.

🥹

Cupping Therapy

Suction cups applied to soft tissue to promote circulation, release fascial restrictions, and reduce muscle soreness and inflammation.

🤺

Soft Tissue Mobilization

Manual techniques targeting muscles, tendons, and ligaments to break down scar tissue, restore movement, and alleviate pain.

🩹

Kinesio Taping

Elastic therapeutic tape applied to support joints and muscles, reduce swelling, and improve proprioception during activity and recovery.

🔧

IASTM (Instrument-Assisted)

Specialized tools used to detect and treat soft tissue dysfunction, scar tissue, and fascial restrictions with precision and effectiveness.

🧻

Spinal Decompression

Traction-based therapy to relieve pressure on spinal discs and nerves. Effective for disc herniations, radiculopathy, and sciatica.

🖐

Joint Mobilizations

Skilled manual techniques to restore joint mechanics, improve range of motion, and reduce pain in stiff or restricted joints.

Florida PTs perform mobilizations, not spinal manipulations.

🏋

Blood Flow Restriction (BFR) Training

A technique using cuffs to restrict venous blood flow, enabling significant strength gains with very low loads. Ideal for early post-surgical rehab.

⬇

Traction

Manual or mechanical traction to decompress spinal structures, reduce nerve root compression, and alleviate radiating pain.
