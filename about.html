About Us

Who We Are

A private practice built on one simple belief: every patient deserves the undivided attention of their therapist, every single session.

The Practice

Private Practice.
Personal Care.

We are a private practice physical therapy clinic offering cash-based services designed to provide high-quality, individualized care without the limitations of insurance. By operating on a cash-based model, we are able to focus entirely on what matters most: your recovery, your goals, and your long-term health.

As the sole owner and sole physical therapist, every patient receives direct, one-on-one physical therapy care from start to finish. There are no assistants, no overlapping appointments, and no rushed sessions. Each visit is personalized to your specific condition, movement needs, and lifestyle demands.

Our goal is to help you move better, feel better, and get back to living the life you want through expert care, education, and hands-on treatment. Here, you are not a number. You are the focus.

Meet Your Therapist

Dr. Travis Kostello,
PT, DPT

Dr. Travis Kostello is a licensed physical therapist with over five years of experience helping patients move better, recover from injuries, and live pain-free. He founded this practice with a simple mission: to provide a more personalized and higher-quality physical therapy experience built on genuine connection, individualized care, and lasting results.

Dr. Kostello earned his Doctor of Physical Therapy (DPT) degree from Rockhurst University in Kansas City, Missouri, after completing his Bachelor of Science in Biology at Iowa Wesleyan University.

Originally from St. Louis, he grew up immersed in sports and developed an early appreciation for the importance of health, movement, and performance. His passion for athletics and firsthand understanding of injury and recovery drives his commitment to helping others achieve their fitness goals and live active, pain-free lives through optimized motion and movement education.

Dr. Kostello dedicates quality time to understanding each patient, asking insightful questions and actively listening to their concerns. This allows him to expertly craft a personalized plan of care designed to precisely meet each patient's individual needs. By establishing a strong rapport, he ensures that patients feel valued and heard, fostering an environment of trust and openness. He empowers patients to take an active role in their own health journey, leading to better outcomes and increased satisfaction with their care.

Outside the clinic, Travis enjoys spending time with his wife and their dogs. Together, they stay active through biking, hiking, working out, and exploring the outdoors. Travis is an avid fan of the Kansas City Chiefs, Iowa Hawkeyes, St. Louis Cardinals, and St. Louis Blues.

Our Mission

At PT Kinetics Physical Therapy and Performance, our mission is to restore functional movement and empower individuals to live pain-free through compassionate, one-on-one care with an emphasis on evidence-based practice. We are committed to providing personalized treatment that supports each patient's unique goals, whether returning to daily life, achieving fitness milestones, or enhancing overall well-being. We aim to elevate the quality of life across every stage of recovery and performance.

🌟

Our Vision

To create a world where every person can move pain-free and live fully, by providing compassionate and personalized care. Our vision is to be a trusted provider of physical therapy and performance training, empowering individuals to overcome pain, restore confidence in movement, and achieve their goals, enabling them to return to the life they love.
