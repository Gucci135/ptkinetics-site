The Practice

Private Practice.
Personal Care.

We are a private practice physical therapy clinic offering cash-based services designed to provide high-quality, individualized care without the limitations of insurance.

As the sole owner and sole physical therapist, every patient receives direct, one-on-one care from start to finish. No assistants, no overlapping appointments, no rushed sessions. Here, you are not a number. You are the focus.

Our Services

Physical Therapy and Rehabilitation

  • General physical therapy and pain management
  • Post-surgical and sports injury rehabilitation
  • Spine care, vestibular, and balance training
  • Headache relief and osteoporosis management
  • Return-to-work programs

Manual Therapy

  • Dry needling
  • Cupping therapy
  • Instrument-assisted soft tissue mobilization (IASTM)
  • Kinesio taping and joint mobilizations
  • Blood flow restriction (BFR) training

Performance and Fitness

  • Athletic performance and sport-specific training
  • Running programs and gait analysis
  • Weightlifting and strength programs
  • Postural assessment and movement analysis
  • Personal training (coming soon)

Lifestyle and Wellness

  • Ergonomic training (work and home setups)
  • Wellness and nutrition education
  • Telehealth visits
  • At-home visits
