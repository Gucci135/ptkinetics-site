Have Insurance?

We Provide
Superbills

A superbill is an itemized receipt of services that includes diagnosis codes and procedure codes. You can submit it directly to your insurance company as a claim for out-of-network reimbursement. If your plan has out-of-network benefits, you may be reimbursed for a portion of the cost.

1

Come in for your session

Pay at the time of service as normal.

2

Receive your superbill

We provide a detailed superbill with all the codes your insurance needs.

3

Submit to your insurer

Submit the superbill to your insurance company and receive reimbursement directly.

💡

Before your first visit

Call your insurance company and ask about your out-of-network physical therapy benefits and deductible. This helps you understand how much you may be reimbursed.

Have questions about the superbill process? Ask us and we will walk you through it.
